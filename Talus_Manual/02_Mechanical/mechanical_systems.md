# Project Talus: Mechanical Configuration
**Status:** Frozen for Build Phase 1
**Platform:** 1988 Ford Bronco II (RBV Chassis)
**Wheelbase:** 94.0" (Stock)

---

## 1. Powertrain Architecture (The "SVT" Heart)
* **Engine:** 5.0L (302ci) Ford Windsor V8.
    * *Source:* 1996-2001 Ford Explorer (GT40P Heads & Intake).
    * *Mounts:* Convertible Motor Mounts (L&L or James Duff) for 302 swap into Ranger/BII.
    * *Headers:* Torque Monster or Shorty swap headers (Critical for steering shaft clearance).
    * *Oil Pan:* Dual-sump Mustang pan (Required to clear TTB crossmember).
* **Transmission:** Mazda M5OD-R2 (5-Speed Manual).
    * *Source:* 1997-2003 Ford F-150 (4.2L V6 or 4.6L V8 bellhousing pattern match).
    * *Shifter Location:* Custom cutout required in tunnel (~3" rearward of stock).
* **Transfer Case:** Advance Adapters Atlas II.
    * *Ratio:* 3.8:1 or 4.3:1 (TBD).
    * *Clocking:* Flat-clocked for ground clearance (requires floor modification).

---

## 2. Suspension & Axles (The "Raptor" Legs)
### Front Suspension (TTB)
* **Axle:** Dana 35 TTB (Twin Traction Beam).
    * *Source:* 1991-1994 Ford Explorer.
    * *Width:* +2" wider per side than stock Bronco II Dana 28.     
    * *Gearing:* 4.10 or 4.56 (Matched to Rear).
    * *Differential:* Open (Selectable Locker planned).
* **Springs:** James Duff Variable Rate Coils (2.5" - 3" Lift).
* **Radius Arms:** Extended Radius Arms (James Duff or similar) for articulation.

### Rear Suspension
* **Axle:** Ford 8.8" (31-Spline).
    * *Source:* 1996-2001 Ford Explorer.
    * *Brakes:* Factory Disc Brakes (Critical upgrade over BII drums).
    * *Width:* Matches Front Dana 35 width perfectly.
    * *Adaptation:* Spring perches must be welded to "Spring Over Axle" (SOA) configuration for Bronco II.
* **Leaf Springs:** 63" Chevy Spring Swap OR James Duff Progressive Pack (TBD).

---

## 3. Steering & Braking
### Steering System
* **Column:** 1998-2003 Ford Ranger Tilt Column.
* **Wheel:** Lincoln Navigator (Wood/Leather).
* **Linkage:** K-Link or Crossover Steering (Required to eliminate bump-steer with TTB lift).
* **Box:** Stock Ford Power Steering Box (tapped for Hydro Assist in Phase 2).

### Braking System
* **Master Cylinder:** 1995+ Explorer Master (Larger bore for 4-wheel discs).
* **ABS:** BMW MK60E5 Standalone Module.
    * *Sensors:* Custom tone rings on Front D35 / Stock tone ring on Rear 8.8.
    * *Plumbing:* All new NiCopp hard lines.

---

## 4. Fabrication Constraints (Wife Contract)
* **Exhaust:** Must be fully welded and quiet (Flowmaster 50 or Borla). No "open header" testing after 8 PM.
* **Vibrations:** Drivetrain angles must be corrected (shims/CV driveshaft) to ensure highway comfort.
